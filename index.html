Design principle

Rugged field energy system.

Designed as a realistic integration platform: certified fuel storage, off-the-shelf fuel-cell hardware, battery buffering, thermal management, and a serviceable enclosure.

Swappable core concept

Modular cartridge ecosystem.

The Core is imagined as a locked, keyed, smart cartridge that docks into the Fuel Module or Basecamp system. The host verifies latch status, sensor status, temperature, and system readiness before operation.
